Welcome

What is this?

The Imdb Game is a web based game which will allow 2 players to connect and play the game head to head with their knowledge of the imdb top 250

Rules

The game has 8 rounds, the players will be required to guess the year in which the movie was released, a wrong answer will result in 3 points being deducted and a correct answer results in 5 points being added.
The user with the most points wins the game!
